Metahamas NFT's

You can own part of the Metahamas. You can buy plots, buildings, vehicles etc. by purchasing the ownership NFT's.

After purchasing a plot, you can change characters, add accessories or knock the building down and replace it with another building.
If you buy multiple adjoining plots you can merge them and build something new across multiple plots.
