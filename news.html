Metahamas NFT's

This is an idea around owning parts of the Metahamas. We're thinking it would be cool to buy plots, buildings, venues etc. - by purchasing the ownership NFT's.

Thereafter, maybe the building owner can organise the events that take place in the space, or we could just use it as a funding model, whereby The Metahamas could organise the events, and the remuneration from say a corporation using the space to hold a virtual retreat would be paid into a smart contract & out to the different NFT holders...
